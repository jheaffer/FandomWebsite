Character Report - Nico:

It ain't no secret that Vergil's quick with a blade and when you see him in action, he's one slick mofo. That's gotta count for something, right? Just think if Nero'd learned to fight from his old man, yeah, I'd like to see that! Shoot, no use crying about it now. Then again, there probably ain't nothing Vergil'd hate more than having to relying on his human side... Talk about issues!
